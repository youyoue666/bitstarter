This is develop branch 2 hahaha QEvaluater (Q means Cute) is a simple website for you to evalute anyone you are interested, which could be Obama, or Oprah, or your boss, or your manager. You don't have to register for account things, which is considered annoying and not necessary to this product. When you arrive the website, first, you could search the person you want to evaluate, if you can't find the person, you can create the profile of this guy; second, just click the up-arrow (suuport) or down-arrow (nonsupport) to denote your attitude to this guy; third, if you like, just add your comments to this guy, maybe the reason why you think he/she should get support or nonsupport.

The profile of a persion being evaluated only consists of his/her job position. The website will be divided into two parts: public and private. If the guy's position is related with the public organization, it will be public; if it is specified related with a private organization, it will be private. If you want to evaluate a guy in the private part, you have to be included in a plan need to pay. Actually, the private part is for a company to organize its employee and their evaluation about each other, which can not be public. At the backend of the website, I will implement specific code to decide which kind of profile could be public or private.
